About

From Nuclear Engineering
to Cybersecurity Leadership

My journey into cybersecurity began in an unlikely place — deep in the nuclear engineering systems of Navy shipbuilding at Huntington Ingalls Industries. Working on submarine reactor instrumentation and control systems taught me something fundamental: when failure isn't an option, you need flawless processes, rigorous compliance, and teams that execute with precision.

Over 12+ years, I've evolved into an enterprise-level leader at the intersection of cybersecurity program management and Agile transformation — securing mission-critical systems across the DoD, Navy, Army, Marine Corps, Joint Staff, NSWDG, and OPM.

I believe the strongest security posture comes when technical discipline meets organizational agility. Whether leading ATO lifecycles, coaching SAFe teams, or driving DevSecOps adoption, my focus is always on creating sustainable, measurable value.

Those lessons carry directly into how I lead teams and attack complex challenges in cybersecurity.
